Hotels in Amsterdam



Hotel & Restaurant Campanile

Hotel & Restaurant Campanile is an informal and compact hotel that is suitable for both business guests as well as tourists. The hotel has an a la carte restaurant, a small bar/lounge and a free parking area. From metro and bus station Gaasperplas, next to the hotel, the centre of Amsterdam can be reached 24 hours a day.

The WestCord Fashion Hotel Amsterdam

The WestCord Fashion Hotel Amsterdam is located across from the World Fashion Centre, just inside the A10 motorway. From the four-star hotel, it’s just a ten-minute tram ride to Amsterdam Airport Schiphol and the RAI Convention Centre. The vibrant Leidseplein and other major attractions are also just 10 minutes away by tram.

Hotel Atlanta

Hotel Atlanta is a traditional hotel in located on the Rembrandtplein in the centre of Amsterdam. Shops, restaurants, museums, canal tours, galleries, bars, cinemas and nightclubs are all within walking distance. You’ll find a taxi stand and tram stop directly in front of the hotel, giving you excellent connections to the rest of the city.

Hotel Alexander

Hotel Alexander is located in the historical heart of Amsterdam and offers all the comforts you would expect from a 3-star hotel. The 32 nicely furnished rooms all have a colour television, private bathroom and telephone. A delicious breakfast buffet is served in the breakfast lounge. There is also a cosy bar and a lounge.

A-Train Hotel

The A-Train Hotel is a unique 3 star hotel, ideally located in front of the Central (Railway) Station in the beating heart of Amsterdam. Breakfast and all taxes are always included in the room prices. Smoking, non-smoking and family rooms available. Shopping areas, tourist attractions, pubs, restaurants, theaters, are all within walking distance.

Hotel Ibis Amsterdam Airport

The modern, three star ibis Schiphol Amsterdam Airport hotel boasts 644 rooms with free wifi, four restaurants, a bar and fitness centre. Easy to reach via the A4 and A9, or on the free airport shuttle service, it only takes 20 minutes on public transport to Amsterdam city centre, or to the RAI Exhibition Centre.

Hotel de Westertoren

Hotel de Westertoren is a family-run hotel in the centre of Amsterdam. From the hotel, it’s a 15 minute-walk to Central Station and a two-minute walk to the Dam. All rooms in this hotel have a private shower, television and coffee and tea making facilities.

The Ibis Amsterdam City West

The Ibis Amsterdam City West is located close to the harbour, between the heart of Amsterdam and the main business districts. The RAI convention centre and Amsterdam Schiphol Airport are easily accessible thanks to the hotel’s proximity to the A10 motorway. Book one of the 180 air-conditioned rooms and benefit from a variety of amenities, including a restaurant, 24-hour bar, terrace area and a private entrance to the public parking area. Business guests will appreciate the Wi-Fi internet access.

The Avenue Hotel

The Avenue Hotel is a three star hotel with 80 modern rooms. Each level can be reached by elevator and the rooms all have a bath or shower, and a toilet. Furthermore, all rooms have WIFI, a television with an internal video channel, a hairdryer and a telephone with a direct outside line. Every morning an American breakfast is served in the restaurant. Apart from the usual communication facilities that are offered at the reception, there are safes and a laundry-service. It is also possible to book excursions at the reception.
