Restaurants in Amsterdam



Mozo

Mr and Mrs. Watson

Mr. & Mrs. Watson wants to show what plant based cuisine really has to offer , to contribute to amsterdam's growing list of vegan restaurants.With a menu offering solely plant based fare,its also heaven for vegans and cheese lovers.

&Moshik

Just a short walk from amsterdam central station , and with two michelin stars to its name , &moshik is the personal creation of top chef , Moshik Roth.Every season brings a new menu in honour of the freshest priduce at the moment.

Jaspers

This restrau serves a three course meal that can be expanded to up tp seven courses . The main menu changes every two weeks and is compiled from fresh , high quality produce . Guests can also chooses one of the starters or main dishes as a special, offered outside of the regular menu.

Mr. Porter

Mr Porter is a new arrival on spuistraat in amsterdam ,a luxury steakhouse on the top floor of upmarket new business hotel.The Butcher ,the restaurants's website enigmatically statesb that " MR PORTER fearlessly explores the borders between dinner and sinner."

Mos Amsterdam

A stone's throw from central station , this fine dining restraurant earned a coveted michelin star in 2016 . The restaurant offers french and international cuisine , working with pure , honest ingredients and plenty of seasonal produce , fish and meat.

Morlang

opens for lunch and dinner seven days a week , this restaurant and bar offers adelightful menu of daily and seasonally changing dishes.Loacated in a classic canal house on amsterdam's keizergracht canal , Morlang boasts a welcoming and authentic atmosphere where diners can enjoy a stylish meal.

Puri Mass

It has been serving authentic indonesian food in acosy setting in the heart of amterdam since 1989 . The decorations of original indonesian handicrafts and paintings and the friendly batil clad staff create a congenial ambience.Puri mass is loacated near leidseplein with its entertainment option.

Mossel and Gin

this restaurant at amsterdam specializes in -you guessed it-mussels and G&T , plus oysters, lobster and other maritime delicacies.With large tables and plenty of dishes to share on its menu ,such as ceviche or a generous seafood platter for two ,also encourages social dining with bunch of friends.
